Usage Guide


How to register for an account

  1. Open the 'register' page open_in_new and sign up for an account.
  2. Enter a unique username and choose your password.
  3. Press the 'register' button to complete your account creation.

How to login to your account

  1. Open the Login page open_in_new
  2. Enter the username and password that you signed up with on the 'register' page.
  3. Press the 'login' button to submit your details.

How to use the dashboard

  • Add an expense:
    • Navigate to the dashboard by logging into your account.
    • Locate the 'add entry' panel on the left side of the screen.
    • Enter the details about the transaction: description, amount & type.
    • Press the 'submit entry' button.
  • Remove an expense:
    • Find the transaction you wish to remove.
    • Open the action menu next to that transaction.
    • Select 'remove transaction'.
